Biography

I’m a Software Engineer working on Network Infrastructure in Google Cloud. My interests span across all of networking; but I spend most of my time thinking about how can we enable our networks to provide even lower-latency and higher-throughput while preserving isolation across a large number of applications that need to be supported at Google.
